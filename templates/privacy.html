Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025  ·  Applies to {{ app_name }} and all its services

Short version: We collect your email, country, and phone number to create your account. Photos you upload are processed by AI and never stored on our servers. We do not sell your data to anyone.

1. Who We Are

{{ app_name }} is an AI-assisted concrete defect analysis tool. We provide preliminary visual inspection reports based on photographs submitted by our users. References to "we", "us", or "our" refer to the operator of this service.

Contact: contact@concretedefectai.com

2. Data We Collect

Account data (collected at registration)

  • Email address — used to identify your account and for communication
  • Password — stored as a one-way cryptographic hash (bcrypt). We cannot read your password.
  • Country — used for regional service compliance
  • Phone number — optional, used for account recovery
  • Consent record — date and confirmation you agreed to this policy

Usage data (automatically collected)

  • Number of analyses performed (tries remaining)
  • Account creation date

What we do NOT collect

  • Photos you upload — processed in memory and immediately discarded. Never written to disk or stored in any database.
  • Reports generated — exist only in your browser session. Not stored on our servers.
  • Cookies, tracking pixels, or advertising identifiers
  • IP addresses or precise location data

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To authenticate you when you sign in
  • To track the number of free analyses used
  • To respond to support or data requests

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing this service.

4. Third-Party AI Services

To generate inspection reports and defect maps, your uploaded photos are transmitted to:

  • Anthropic (claude.ai) — for structured report generation
  • Google (Gemini API) — for defect map image generation

These providers process images only to respond to the API request. Neither provider stores images after processing. Refer to their privacy policies for full details.

We do not share any account data (email, phone, country) with these providers.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Account data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted in a secured cloud environment
  • All connections are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and cannot be reversed
  • Authentication tokens expire after 30 days
  • Photos are never written to disk — they exist only in server memory during processing

6. Your Rights (GDPR & Global)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of your stored data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Right to object — object to processing of your data
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a structured format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@concretedefectai.com. We will respond within 30 days. You can also delete your account instantly from within the application.

7. Data Retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently and immediately removed from our database.

8. Children

This service is intended for professional use by adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, contact us for immediate deletion.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
