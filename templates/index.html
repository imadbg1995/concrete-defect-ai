Defect Classification

Color-coded by crack width

Every crack is visually classified by its measured width. The overlay uses four distinct colors so severity is instantly readable on-site.

COLOR CATEGORY WIDTH THRESHOLD TYPICAL CAUSE
Cyan
Hairline / Fine < 0.5 mm Shrinkage, thermal movement, early-age stress
Yellow
Medium crack 0.5 – 2 mm Structural loading, ASR, carbonation-induced
Orange
Wide crack > 2 mm Advanced structural distress, rebar corrosion, overload
Magenta
Spalling / Void Open zone Delamination, freeze-thaw damage, rebar corrosion pressure
Width classification is performed visually by our AI engine. All results must be verified on-site by a licensed structural engineer. This tool does not replace professional inspection.
